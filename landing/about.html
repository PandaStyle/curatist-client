About The Curatist

The modern way to browse
architectural sources.

Keep ahead&stay inspired.

A single platform to browse all the architectural news that keeps you ahead and inspired. The Curatist aggregates and filters quality content from around the web and presents it to you in a clean, organized and effortless way.

See the latest news, trends and updates from your favorite websites and beyond. Overview the entire AEC industry at one glance or choose your focus on Design, Tech and Business. Explore the inspiration board and use smart search to save time.

Available online, mobile and as a new-tab extension for Chrome.
